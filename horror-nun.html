About Horror Nun

Survive the nightmare in Horror Nun! Escape a haunted school while avoiding a terrifying antagonist in this intense first-person stealth horror game.

What is Horror Nun

Horror Nun is a spine-chilling survival horror game set within the dark confines of an abandoned religious school. You play as a student trapped inside who is being hunted by a demonic nun who can hear every sound you make. The gameplay focuses heavily on stealth, exploration, and complex puzzle-solving. You must find hidden items and keys to unlock new areas while staying completely out of sight. The atmosphere is built on extreme tension and sudden scares, making it a thrilling experience for fans of the horror genre.

How to Play Horror Nun

Move Use the joystick or WASD keys to move silently through the hallways while avoiding creaky floorboards.

Hide Interact with lockers or crawlspaces to hide when you hear the nun approaching your location.

Solve Collect items like keys and tools to solve environmental puzzles that block your escape routes.

Listen Pay close attention to audio cues since the sound of footsteps will tell you how close you are to danger.
